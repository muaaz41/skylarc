Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about selling assets with Skylarc, including how auctions work, pricing, timelines, and what to expect throughout the process.

Skylarc specialises in selling machinery, vehicles, industrial plant, tools, and business equipment through online auctions.

Skylarc specialises in machinery, vehicles, industrial plant, tools, and business equipment, sold through professionally managed online auctions across New Zealand.

How are assets sold?

Assets are sold through online event-style auctions, allowing buyers to place bids over a set auction period until the highest bid wins.

Where are the auctions hosted?

Auctions are hosted on Skylarc's online auction platform, where registered buyers can browse catalogues, place bids, and track activity in real time.

Who can participate in the auctions?

Any verified buyer who registers on the platform can participate, including businesses, contractors, resellers, and private buyers depending on asset type.

How do I sell assets through Skylarc?

Start by contacting our team with your asset details. We then assess the assets, recommend a strategy, prepare listings, market the auction, and manage settlement.

How quickly can assets be sold through an online auction?

Timing depends on asset type and campaign scope, but most auctions are prepared and launched within days, with sale outcomes achieved within the scheduled auction window.

What types of businesses typically sell assets through Skylarc?

We regularly support construction, transport, manufacturing, agriculture, and engineering businesses, along with insolvency and business closure asset sales.

Why use an online auction instead of a private sale?

Online auctions create transparent competition between buyers, improve market reach, and often generate stronger pricing outcomes than one-to-one private negotiations.
